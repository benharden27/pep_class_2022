Phytoplankton are the base of many food webs in the ocean. They are single-celled plant-like organisms that typically get their energy from sunlight. They exists throughout the world’s oceans, primarily in the upper sunlit portion.

In this topic we are going to familiarize ourselves with phytoplankton and explore some of the factors that affect their abundance and geographic distribution.
