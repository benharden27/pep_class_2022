Let’s apply some of these idea to a global map of phytoplankton distribution. Green/yellow/red colors indicate more pythoplankton and blue/purple show less phytoplankton.

Prepare for a group discussion about this figure. Think about:

  1. Where are phytoplankton found in large amounts? Where are there fewer?

  2. Which of the processes described above could explain the patterns you are seeing in different regions?
