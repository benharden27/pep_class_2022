In the level 3 discussions you argued for a particular topic to be the recipient of the prestigious Harden prize. You also heard form your peers and found synergies between your topics.

Your task now is to create a one-page proposal for the work you are going to undertake to make an impact on how well we understand the process. You should think about the following:

  • What is not well known? What do you want to know?
  • What do you want to find out?
  • What do you need to measure?
  • Where do you want to measure it?
  • What other resources will you need?
  • What difficulties do you envision in executing your plan?
